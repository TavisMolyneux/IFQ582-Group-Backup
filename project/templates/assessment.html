Item Assessment: Bark Painting Collection

⏳ In Progress

Item Details

Series of bark paintings from Arnhem Land artists depicting seasonal knowledge. The collection includes five paintings created between 1975 and 1990, each representing different aspects of seasonal change and land management practices.

Item ID: 3
Date Added: 10 March 2026
Format: Bark painting (digitised photograph)
Dimensions: 85cm x 60cm (each)
Location: Special Collections — Room 5, Cabinet A
Assessment Initiated: 15 March 2026
Cultural Metadata
Discussion
Uncle Jim Mooney 18 March 2026

I need to consult with the Kunwinjku community before making a determination. The seasonal knowledge depicted in these paintings may have specific protocols for sharing.

Aunty May Williams 20 March 2026

I agree with Uncle Jim. I have seen similar paintings shared in educational contexts, but we should confirm with the community first. The land management practices shown may also need context to be understood properly by a general audience.
