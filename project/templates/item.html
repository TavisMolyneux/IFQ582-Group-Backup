Dreaming Story Scroll

Description

A hand-painted scroll depicting a creation narrative from the Wiradjuri people. This scroll illustrates the journey of ancestral beings across Country and has been used in community education programs. The artwork uses traditional ochre pigments and was created by a Wiradjuri elder in the 1980s. It was donated to the NIKL collection in 2024 with the permission of the elder's family.
